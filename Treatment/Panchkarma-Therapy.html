Panchakarma Therapy

About Panchakarma Therapy

Panchakarma is one of the most profound detoxification therapies in Ayurveda, designed to remove toxins from the body, balance the doshas, and rejuvenate the entire system. At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer personalized Panchakarma treatments that address your individual health needs. Our therapies are designed to cleanse the body, improve vitality, and restore harmony to your mind, body, and spirit.

Panchakarma Process

  • Vamana (Therapeutic Vomiting): Removes excess mucus from the stomach and respiratory tract.
  • Virechana (Purgation): Detoxifies the body by removing excess bile and toxins from the digestive tract.
  • Nasya (Nasal Therapy): Aids in clearing toxins from the head and sinuses, enhancing brain function.
  • Basti (Enema Therapy): A detox therapy that cleanses the colon, replenishing the body with vital fluids.
  • Raktamokshana (Bloodletting): Used for purifying the blood and removing toxins from the body.

Benefits of Panchakarma Therapy

  • Detoxification of the body and mind
  • Balances the doshas and improves overall health
  • Improves digestion and metabolism
  • Enhances immunity and rejuvenates the body
  • Promotes stress reduction and mental clarity
  • Helps in weight management and skin glow
