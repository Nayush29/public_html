PCOD and Endometriosis Treatment

About PCOD and Endometriosis Treatment

PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease) and Endometriosis are common hormonal disorders affecting women. At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer personalized Ayurvedic treatments that address the root causes of these conditions. Our treatments focus on restoring hormonal balance, improving fertility, and alleviating symptoms such as pain, irregular cycles, and hormonal imbalance.

Treatment Methods

  • Herbal Remedies: Tailored Ayurvedic herbs that regulate hormones and improve ovarian function.
  • Diet and Lifestyle Adjustments: Personalized dietary and lifestyle plans to balance the body’s hormones and improve metabolic function.
  • Panchakarma Detox: Detoxification therapies that cleanse the reproductive system and support hormonal health.
  • Stress Management: Techniques like Yoga and Meditation to manage stress and promote hormonal balance.
  • Ayurvedic Massages: Therapeutic massages to improve circulation and reproductive health.

Benefits of Ayurvedic Treatment for PCOD and Endometriosis

  • Regulates menstrual cycles and reduces irregularities
  • Reduces ovarian cysts and promotes healthy ovulation
  • Balances hormonal levels and reduces symptoms like acne and facial hair growth
  • Improves fertility and reproductive health
  • Reduces pain and inflammation associated with Endometriosis
  • Detoxifies the body and reduces the accumulation of toxins
