About Allergy and Respiratory Disorders

Allergy and respiratory disorders include conditions such as asthma, hay fever, chronic bronchitis, and allergic rhinitis, which affect the respiratory system. These conditions are often triggered by environmental allergens like dust, pollen, and pet dander. Ayurvedic treatments focus on strengthening the immune system, reducing inflammation, and clearing respiratory pathways for better breathing.
