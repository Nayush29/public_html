About Neuro-Ortho Care

At Ayurmax Hospital, Dr. Arvind Chaudhry offers specialized Neuro-Ortho Care combining the expertise in both neurological and orthopedic fields. Whether it’s a neurological disorder affecting the nervous system or orthopedic concerns related to joints, bones, and muscles, we provide holistic, personalized treatments aimed at restoring function, reducing pain, and enhancing overall health. Through a combination of Ayurvedic therapies, advanced diagnostics, and modern treatment options, we focus on a comprehensive approach to Neuro-Ortho health.

Treatment Options

  • Ayurvedic Herbal Treatments for Neurological and Orthopedic Conditions
  • Panchakarma Detoxification Therapy
  • Customized Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Plans
  • Stress Reduction and Pain Management through Yoga
  • Personalized Nutrition and Lifestyle Counseling

Words from Dr. Arvind Chaudhry

"Neuro-Ortho health requires a multidisciplinary approach, and Ayurveda offers powerful, natural remedies to address these complex issues. At Ayurmax, we carefully analyze each patient’s condition and create a tailored treatment plan to help restore optimal function and improve quality of life.
