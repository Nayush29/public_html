Autoimmune Diseases

About Autoimmune Diseases

Autoimmune diseases occur when the immune system mistakenly attacks the body’s own tissues. Common conditions include rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, and psoriasis. Ayurvedic treatments aim to balance the immune response, detoxify the body, and reduce inflammation, promoting overall health and wellness.

Treatments Available

  • Ayurvedic Herbal Therapies for Immune Modulation
  • Panchakarma Therapy for Detoxification
  • Dietary and Lifestyle Guidance for Symptom Management
  • Yoga and Meditation for Mind-Body Balance
