About Male and Female Infertility Treatment

Infertility can affect both men and women and may arise from various factors. At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer customized Ayurvedic treatments for male and female infertility that target the root causes of reproductive health issues. Using a combination of herbal remedies, detoxification therapies, diet modifications, and stress management techniques, we aim to restore fertility and improve overall reproductive health naturally.

Treatments Available

  • Herbal Remedies for Hormonal Balance and Reproductive Health
  • Detoxification Therapies for Cleansing and Enhancing Fertility
  • Personalized Nutrition Plans to Support Reproductive Function
  • Yoga and Pranayama for Stress Reduction and Health Promotion
  • Male and Female Fertility Enhancement Programs
