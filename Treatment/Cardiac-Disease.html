Cardiac Disease Treatment

About Cardiac Disease Treatment

Cardiac diseases such as hypertension, coronary artery disease, and arrhythmias are conditions that require comprehensive care. At Ayurmax Hospital, our Ayurvedic approach emphasizes lifestyle modification, herbal remedies, and therapies that improve heart function and reduce stress. These treatments are designed to prevent disease progression and enhance overall cardiovascular health.

Treatments Available

  • Ayurvedic Herbs for Cardiovascular Support
  • Panchakarma Therapy for Detoxification
  • Yoga and Pranayama for Heart Health
  • Diet and Lifestyle Counseling for Blood Pressure Management

Words from Dr. Arvind Chaudhry

"The heart is not just an organ but the center of life. At Ayurmax, we focus on treating cardiac diseases with a holistic approach, ensuring that our patients receive treatments that enhance their heart health while also improving their overall wellness. With Ayurveda, we aim to provide long-lasting solutions for a healthy heart."
