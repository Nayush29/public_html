About Gynecology Care

At Ayurmax Hospital, we specialize in providing holistic and personalized Ayurvedic treatments for women's health. Our gynecology care addresses a wide range of issues including menstrual disorders, hormonal imbalances, infertility, and menopause. Our therapies are designed to promote overall wellness and restore harmony in the body.

Treatment Options Available

  • Menstrual Disorder Management
  • PCOD and Endometriosis Treatment
  • Infertility Treatment
  • Hormonal Imbalance Correction
  • Menopause Support and Care
