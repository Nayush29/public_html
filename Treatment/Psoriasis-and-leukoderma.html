Psoriasis & Leukoderma Treatment

About Psoriasis & Leukoderma Treatment

Psoriasis and Leukoderma (Vitiligo) are chronic skin conditions that cause distressing symptoms like patches, dryness, and discoloration. At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer Ayurvedic solutions that help manage and reduce these conditions by restoring the balance of the body and promoting healthy skin. Our holistic approach includes natural treatments, customized skincare regimens, and detox therapies designed to rejuvenate the skin and treat underlying imbalances.

Treatment Methods

  • Herbal Remedies: Natural Ayurvedic herbs that promote skin healing and reduce inflammation.
  • Topical Treatments: Special Ayurvedic oils and creams that help improve skin texture and reduce skin lesions.
  • Detoxification Programs: Panchakarma therapies that cleanse the body and improve skin health from within.
  • Diet and Lifestyle Adjustments: A personalized diet plan to nourish the skin and reduce toxins.
  • Therapeutic Massage: Ayurvedic oil massages that enhance skin regeneration and circulation.

Benefits of Ayurvedic Treatment for Psoriasis & Leukoderma

  • Reduces the formation of skin plaques and patches
  • Promotes the regeneration of healthy skin cells
  • Reduces inflammation and redness associated with Psoriasis
  • Restores pigmentation and reduces depigmented patches in Leukoderma
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Strengthens the immune system to prevent flare-ups

Words from Dr. Arvind Chaudhry

"Psoriasis and Leukoderma can significantly impact a person's quality of life, but Ayurvedic treatments can provide relief and manage the symptoms effectively. Our approach works by addressing the root cause, improving skin health, and providing holistic wellness. We combine natural remedies with lifestyle adjustments to bring long-term healing."
