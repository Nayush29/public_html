About Tuberculosis (TB) Care

Tuberculosis (TB) is a serious infectious disease that primarily affects the lungs. It can be a chronic illness, requiring long-term care and treatment. Ayurmax Hospital offers holistic TB care combining modern medical approaches with Ayurvedic treatments that focus on boosting immunity, improving lung health, and supporting overall recovery. With the right care and support, TB can be effectively managed, leading to improved quality of life and complete recovery.

Available Treatments for TB

  • Ayurvedic Herbal Remedies to support immune health
  • Lung Detoxification Programs
  • Customized Diet and Nutrition Plans for Lung Health
  • Breathing Exercises and Pranayama Techniques
  • Detoxification and Strengthening Therapies
