About Laser Ksharsutra Therapy

Laser Ksharsutra Therapy is a pioneering approach that combines the time-tested Ayurvedic technique of Ksharsutra with modern laser technology. This treatment is especially effective for anorectal disorders such as piles, fistula, and fissures. It ensures minimal pain, faster recovery, and long-lasting results.

Conditions Treated with Laser Ksharsutra

  • Piles (Hemorrhoids)
  • Fistula in Ano
  • Anal Fissures
  • Pilonidal Sinus
  • Chronic Anal Pain
