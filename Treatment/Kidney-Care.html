About Kidney Care

At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer specialized Ayurvedic treatments for kidney health that aim to support, strengthen, and restore the function of the kidneys. With a personalized approach, our treatment plans target the root causes of kidney issues using natural remedies, detoxification methods, and lifestyle adjustments to promote kidney health and overall wellness.

Treatments Available

  • Herbal Kidney Support Remedies
  • Detoxification and Cleansing Programs
  • Customized Diet Plans for Kidney Health
  • Stress Management and Lifestyle Modifications
  • Kidney-Specific Panchakarma Treatment
