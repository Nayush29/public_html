About Gastro-Urinary Care

Digestive and urinary health are essential for overall well-being. At Ayurmax Hospital, we offer holistic Ayurvedic treatments to manage and resolve a wide range of gastro-intestinal and urinary conditions. Our approach involves herbal remedies, dietary adjustments, and detoxification therapies that restore balance and ensure long-term health.

Available Treatments

  • Herbal Remedies for Indigestion and Acidity
  • Detoxification Therapies for Gastrointestinal Health
  • Treatments for Urinary Tract Infections (UTI)
  • Kidney Stone Management and Prevention
  • Custom Diet and Lifestyle Modifications
