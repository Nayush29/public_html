Swarna Prashan

About Swarna Prashan

Swarna Prashan is an ancient Ayurvedic practice that involves administering a special preparation of gold in a medicated form to children, especially in their formative years. This treatment enhances immunity, promotes cognitive development, boosts physical strength, and supports overall health. It is traditionally recommended to boost the mental and physical development of children and protect them from various diseases.

Benefits of Swarna Prashan

  • Boosts immunity and enhances natural defense mechanisms.
  • Improves memory, concentration, and cognitive abilities.
  • Promotes healthy physical growth and development.
  • Helps in preventing common childhood ailments.
  • Increases vitality and energy levels in children.
  • Supports healthy skin and a glowing complexion.

Words from Dr. Rekha Chaudhary

"Swarna Prashan is an invaluable gift of Ayurveda for children's overall well-being. It is not just about improving immunity; it’s about nurturing the child's body and mind to ensure their optimal growth. We at Ayurmax ensure that every Swarna Prashan therapy is administered with the utmost care, using only the finest quality preparations."
