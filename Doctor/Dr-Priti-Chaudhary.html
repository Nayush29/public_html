About Dr. Priti Chaudhary

Dr. Priti Chaudhary is a highly skilled Ayurvedic Dermatologist and Cosmetologist at Ayurmax Hospital, Dehradun. She specializes in skincare and haircare treatments, focusing on both treating disorders and preserving the natural beauty of individuals. She has pioneered Ayurvedic Medi-facials and skin detoxification techniques for effective, holistic skin and hair care.

Education & Qualifications

  • B.A.M.S. from MUHS, Nasik, Maharashtra
  • M.D. (Ayu) from HNB Garhwal University, Uttarakhand
  • Diploma Certification in Medical Aesthetics (D.M.A.) from Mumbai

Expertise

Dr. Priti Chaudhary specializes in a wide range of hair and skin disorders, offering treatments for:

  • Hair:
    • Hair Loss / Hair Fall
    • Dandruff and Itchy Scalp
    • Premature Graying of Hair
    • Hair Thinning
    • Dry and Frizzy Hair
    • Split Ends
    • Scalp Psoriasis
    • Seborrheic Dermatitis
    • Alopecia Areata
  • Skin:
    • Melasma (Blemishes)
    • Acne Vulgaris (Pimples)
    • Under Eye Dark Circles
    • Anti-aging Treatments
    • Dry Skin
    • Viral Warts
    • Moles
    • Corns
    • Milia

Innovative Treatment Methods

Dr. Priti employs traditional Ayurvedic techniques with modern skincare treatments, including:

  • Ayurvedic Medi-Facials: Tailored facials using Ayurvedic ingredients for deep skin detoxification and nourishment.
  • Skin Detoxification: Advanced Ayurvedic detox therapies for glowing and healthy skin.
