About Dr. Rekha Chaudhary

Dr. Rekha Chaudhary is a renowned Ayurvedic physician and anorectal surgeon, currently serving as the Director and Chief Physician at Ayurmax Multispeciality Ayurveda Hospital and Panchakarma Centre in Dehradun. With expertise in anorectal diseases, Ksharsutra therapy, and gynecology, Dr. Rekha provides holistic care, blending traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern medical techniques. Her dedication to patient care and her commitment to academic excellence have earned her numerous accolades in the healthcare field.

Education & Qualifications

  • B.A.M.S. from S.D.M college of Ayurveda, Karnataka from RGUHS – 1996
  • M.S. from Ashwini Ayurveda College, Davangere – 2002
  • Fellowship in Anorectal diseases and Ksharsutra from Manipal University

Expertise

Dr. Rekha Chaudhary specializes in treating a variety of complex medical conditions using Ayurveda and Ksharsutra treatments, including:

  • Anorectal Disorders: Internal Hemorrhoids, Fissures, Fistulas
  • Ksharsutra Therapy: A unique treatment for anorectal diseases
  • Gynecology and Women's Health: Holistic treatment for female reproductive health
  • Panchakarma: Detoxification and rejuvenation therapies

Innovative Treatment Methods

Dr. Rekha combines advanced Ayurvedic treatments with specialized techniques for effective outcomes:

  • Anorectal Surgery & Ksharsutra Therapy: Holistic approaches to manage and treat anorectal disorders, including internal hemorrhoids and fistulas.
  • Panchakarma: A comprehensive Ayurvedic detoxification process that improves overall health and treats chronic conditions.
  • Gynecology Treatments: Tailored Ayurvedic solutions for women's reproductive health, including menstrual disorders and postnatal care.
