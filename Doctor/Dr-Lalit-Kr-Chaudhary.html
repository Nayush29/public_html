About Dr. Lalit Kr. Chaudhary

Dr. Lalit Kr. Chaudhary is a skilled anorectal surgeon, Ksharsutra specialist, and gastroenterologist at Ayurmax Hospital, Dehradun. He is well known for his expertise in treating anorectal disorders such as fistulas, hemorrhoids, and pilonidal sinuses, as well as gastrointestinal diseases. Dr. Lalit has also developed a unique approach to treat urethral stricture and benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) using Uttar Basti treatment. He combines the best of Ayurvedic science with modern technologies like LASER and BEIM to provide effective treatments.

Education & Qualifications

  • M.S. (Ayu) from SDM College of Ayurveda, Udupi, Karnataka (2007)
  • Fellowship in Anorectal Diseases and Ksharsutra from Manipal University
  • Certified training in BEIM and LASER Proctology

Expertise

Dr. Lalit Kr. Chaudhary specializes in a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Anorectal diseases: Fistula-in-Ano, Pilonidal Sinus, Perianal Abscess, Fissure-in-Ano, Hemorrhoids (Piles), Anal Polyps, Rectal Prolapse
  • Gastroenterological disorders: Constipation, Hyperacidity, IBS, Colitis, Crohn’s Disease
  • Urinary diseases: Urethral Stricture, BPH, Kidney Stones
  • Chronic Non-healing Wounds: Varicose Ulcers, Diabetic Foot

Innovative Treatment Methods

Dr. Lalit combines traditional Ayurvedic methods with cutting-edge technology:

  • LASER Proctology: A minimally invasive treatment for hemorrhoids, fissures, fistulas, pilonidal sinus, and polyps with minimal pain, blood loss, and scarring.
  • Ksharsutra Therapy: A nonsurgical treatment for anorectal ailments like fistula-in-ano, hemorrhoids, and pilonidal sinus, with fewer complications and a high success rate.
  • Uttar Basti: A specialized treatment for urethral stricture and BPH that utilizes Ayurvedic formulations for effective results.

Career Highlights
